"दे॒वस्य॑ त्वा सवि॒तुᳶ प्र॑स॒व" इत्या॑ह॒ प्रसू᳚त्यै ।

<details><summary>Keith</summary>

'On the impulse of the god Savitr thee', he says, for impelling.
</details>
