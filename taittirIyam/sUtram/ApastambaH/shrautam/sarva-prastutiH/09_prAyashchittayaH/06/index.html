सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. If after one has run (towards the Āhavanīya) the milk meant for the Agnihotra-ritual spills, then having sat down there (having kept the milk-pot there), having taken the milk again o the ladle) one should offer the same (milk) of whatever Quantity it is, then having milked another (cow) one should offer again. Then having recited a verse,1 addressed to Varuṇa one should offer (a libation of ghee)2 with a verse3 addressed to Varuṇa.
