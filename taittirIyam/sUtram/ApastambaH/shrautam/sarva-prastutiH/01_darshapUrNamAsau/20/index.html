11 अद्भिः कपालं संस्पर्श्य ...{Loading}...

अद्भिः कपालं संस्पर्श्य
प्रज्ञातं +++(रक्षःकपालम्?)+++ निधायाप उपस्पृश्य +++(रक्षस्कर्मत्वात्)+++

“देवो वः सविता हिरण्यपाणिः प्रतिगृह्णात्वि"ति पात्र्यां तण्डुलान् प्रस्कन्दयित्वा
“अदब्धेन वश्चक्षुषावपश्यामि रायस्पोषाय वर्चसे!

थिते
  1. Having caused the potsherd to be touched by means of water, having kept it on some marked place, having touched water, with vāyur vo vivinaktu…1 having separated the husked grains (out of the unhusked ones), with devo vaḥ savitā hiraṇyapāṇiḥ pratigr̥hṇātu2 having poured the grains in a vessel, with adabdhena vaścakṣuṣāvapasyāmi…3 having looked at them, he orders (the preparer of the oblation material) with triṣphalīkartavai (to be pounded thrice)..4
