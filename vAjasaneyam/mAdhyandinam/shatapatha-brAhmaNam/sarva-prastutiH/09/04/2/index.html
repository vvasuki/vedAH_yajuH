  1. And as to why he says, ‘Heaven-like heat, hail! heaven-like flame, hail!’–these indeed are names of this fire: he thereby gratifies these, and by the offering he makes them a deity; for only that one is a deity to whom an oblation is offered, but not that to whom it is not offered. Moreover, by naming them, he thus places them on this fire-altar.
